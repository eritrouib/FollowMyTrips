Rome, Italy

Rome needs more than a few days and most people give it fewer than that.

Quick notes

Stay in Trastevere not the centre. Walk everywhere. Book the Borghese Gallery months ahead or you will not get in. Avoid eating anywhere that has photographs on the menu.
