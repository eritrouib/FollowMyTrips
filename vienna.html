Vienna
← All cities

I have been once and have more to write about it. The short version: the museums are extraordinary, the food is good, and the city runs very smoothly.

What I know so far

Getting around

Buy a Vienna City Card for public transport. Covers U-Bahn, trams and buses for the duration.

Where to eat: a personal recommendation

I found this place and loved it. Great pumpkin soup, very good goulash, and of course schnitzel. We were lucky to get a table without a reservation but check ahead on busy days. Find it on Google Maps.

Schnitzel note

Eat Wiener Schnitzel at Figlmuller on Wollzeile, not the tourist branch near the opera.

Full writeup in progress. The Kunsthistorisches Museum needs at least three hours and will have its own section.
