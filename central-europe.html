🗺 Visited

Vienna, Austria

I have been once. The museums are extraordinary, the food is good, and the city runs very smoothly.

Getting around

Buy a Vienna City Card for public transport. Covers U-Bahn, trams and buses.

Where to eat

I found a great place for pumpkin soup, goulash and schnitzel. We were lucky to get a table without a reservation but check ahead on busy days. Find it on Google Maps.

Schnitzel note

Eat Wiener Schnitzel at Figlmuller on Wollzeile, not the tourist branch near the opera.

🗺 Visited

Budapest, Hungary

Still one of the best value cities in Europe given how beautiful it is. Good for families, good for a trip with friends, I have done both.

Thermal baths

Not a tourist gimmick, locals go too. Szechenyi is the most famous but Rudas or Lukacs are less crowded.

For Sale Pub

A colleague recommended this and it was genuinely excellent. The walls are covered in notes and drawings left by visitors, ours should still be there. Live music at weekends. More about it or map.
