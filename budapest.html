Budapest
← All cities

Budapest is still one of the best value cities in Europe given how beautiful it is. Good for families, good for a trip with friends, I have done both.

What I know so far

For Sale Pub

A colleague recommended this and it was genuinely excellent. The walls are covered in notes and drawings left by visitors over the years, ours should still be there somewhere. Live music at weekends, a rock night that was very good when I went. A proper pub, not a tourist bar. More about it here or find it on Google Maps.

Thermal baths

Not a tourist gimmick, locals go too. Szechenyi is the most famous but Rudas or Lukacs are less crowded. Get on the trams, they go everywhere.
